<div class="software-meta">
<h2><code>arcs</code></h2>
<p class="software-tagline">Automated Reactions for CO<sub>2</sub> Storage</p>
<p class="software-description">A package for studying impurity reactions in CO<sub>2</sub> capture streams using stochastic sampling of DFT-based reaction networks.</p>
<p class="software-note">Collaboration with Equinor, TotalEnergies, and Shell.</p>
<p class="software-links"><a href="https://github.com/badw/arcs.git">Original repository</a><span class="software-sep">|</span><a href="https://github.com/equinor/arcs">Equinor fork</a></p>
</div>
</article>
